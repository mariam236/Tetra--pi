Esma Egyptian society

The aim of 'Esma Egptian society for mercy to animals' depends mainly on finding an appropriate shelter for street animals that suffer from being abused, lack of food and maybe locked down in cages. Esma carries campaigns to collect those animals and search for those goodwill ambassadors that can join and be a volunteer and save such pure animals' souls from abusing. So, if you're able to have a pet and kindly take care of, don't hesitate to contact us and join Esma's Facebook page.
